ZEN MARKET
// Gadget Shop — Inventory & E-Commerce System

Zen Market is a web-based gadget and PC component shop built to make buying computer parts easy and accessible. Browse through our full catalog of processors, graphics cards, RAM, storage devices, and more — all from the comfort of your device.

We currently offer Cash on Delivery (COD) as our payment method, meaning you only pay when your order arrives at your doorstep. No upfront payments required!

Cash on Delivery (COD) — How it Works
1. Browse and add items to your cart.
2. Place your order — no payment needed yet.
3. We process and ship your order within 1–3 days.
4. Receive your package and pay the courier the exact amount.
5. Click "Order Received" in Track Orders to confirm delivery.
6. Leave a review to help other shoppers!

Confirm Order Received
⚠️ Important — Please Read Carefully
Only click "Confirm Received" if you have physically received and inspected your item(s).

Once confirmed, this action cannot be undone. This is your confirmation that the order has been delivered and payment was made to the courier.

If you have not yet received your item, please do NOT confirm — this protects your right to a refund or dispute.
